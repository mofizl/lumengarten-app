Hedge Sprite Sheet Extraction Guide
==================================

Grid Layout: 6 columns Ã— 6 rows
Each tile: ~128x128 pixels
Total image: 768x768 pixels

Seasonal Rows:
- Row 1-3: Spring/Summer variations (bright green)
- Row 4-5: Autumn (orange/brown)
- Row 6: Winter (icy blue)

Column Types (left to right):
1. Straight hedge
2. Corner piece
3. T-junction
4. Crossroads/intersection
5. Path/straight path
6. Dead end
